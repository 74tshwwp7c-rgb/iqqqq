The problem

Many talented students in villages have ambition, but not equal access to guidance. They often miss deadlines, don't know where to start, and study alone without feedback.

The gap is not talent — it is navigation, motivation, and support in the right moment.

Access gap

Students need mentorship and clear action plans, not just information.

Time pressure

Application windows are short and easy to miss without reminders.

Low confidence

Students stop applying when they don't see quick progress.

The solution: Qadam

Qadam turns confusing educational pathways into a daily, step-by-step journey. It is designed to work on low-bandwidth devices and keeps students moving forward with small, achievable tasks.

Core MVP features

Opportunity Feed

Curated scholarships, olympiads, and camps with clear eligibility and deadline tags.

7-Day Study Sprint

Bite-sized daily tasks for exam preparation with progress tracking and streaks.

Mentor-style Assistant

Quick question prompts with pre-written guidance to simulate coaching support.

Deadline Alerts

In-app reminders so students never miss an application due date again.

How it works

  1. Step 1: Student chooses goal (ENT, scholarship, olympiad).
  2. Step 2: Qadam generates a weekly action plan.
  3. Step 3: Student completes daily tasks and tracks streak.
  4. Step 4: Platform surfaces matching opportunities and deadlines.
  5. Step 5: Student submits stronger applications with confidence.

Social impact

Qadam helps convert hidden potential into real outcomes by giving rural students the same strategic support urban students often receive.

  • Higher application completion rates
  • Better exam consistency through habits
  • More first-generation students entering top programs
